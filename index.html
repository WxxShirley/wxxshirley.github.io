Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Hong CHENG. Previously, I received my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science from Fudan University in 2021 and 2024, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Yun XIONG.

My research focuses on developing efficient algorithms for Structured Data Analysis and Information Retrieval. I am particularly interested in optimizing how systems organize web contexts and process complex queries in multi-step search scenarios. Prior to my current focus, I conducted research on Graph Learning.
